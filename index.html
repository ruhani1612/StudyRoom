Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about FocusHive

How does the AI Study Buddy work?
+

Our AI Study Buddy uses advanced machine learning to provide personalized study recommendations, answer questions, and keep you motivated throughout your study sessions. It learns from your study patterns to offer increasingly relevant support.

Is FocusHive really free to use?
+

Yes! FocusHive's core features are completely free. This includes study rooms, basic analytics, and community features. We also offer Premium plans with advanced AI features and unlimited storage for power users.

Can I study offline?
+

While many features require internet connection for collaboration, you can use our Pomodoro timers, goal tracking, and personal notes offline. Your data syncs automatically when you reconnect.

How do I join study rooms?
+

Simply browse our available study rooms by subject or study type, and click "Join Room." You can also create your own custom study room and invite friends to join your focused study environment.

Is my study data secure and private?
+

Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade encryption to protect your data and never share your personal study information. You have complete control over your privacy settings and what you choose to share with the community.
